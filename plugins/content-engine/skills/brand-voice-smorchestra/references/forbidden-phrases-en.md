# Forbidden Phrases — English

## Filler Openers — Delete on sight
- "In today's rapidly evolving landscape"
- "Now more than ever"
- "As we navigate"
- "In the world of [topic]"
- "It's important to note that"
- "I wanted to share"

## Vague Power Words — Replace with specific language
- Leverage → use / apply / run / deploy
- Synergy → name the actual combination
- Ecosystem → market / stack / network
- Holistic → list what's included
- Digital transformation → say what actually changed
- Innovative / cutting-edge / world-class / best-in-class → give the metric

## Guru Content — Deleted on sight
- "Transform your business"
- "Unlock your potential"
- "The secret is..."
- "Game-changer"
- "Disruptive"
- "Revolutionary"

## Hedging — Kills authority
- "It depends" (without specifics)
- "On one hand... on the other hand"
- "There are many factors to consider"
- "Results may vary"

## AI Tells — Signals unedited generation
- "Dive into" / "Delve into"
- "Navigating the complexities of"
- "Harness the potential"
- "In the realm of"
- "Shed light on"
- "Needless to say"
- "Last but not least"
- "Without further ado"
- "I hope this helps"
- "Feel free to"
- Excessive: "crucial," "vital," "essential," "key"
