# Gemini Prompts — Supporting Visuals for Carousel Slides

Use these prompts to generate illustrations/images to place INSIDE each slide alongside the text.

## Style Prefix (add to every prompt)

```
Minimal flat illustration on pure black background. Orange (#FF6600) and white only — no other colors. Simple geometric shapes, clean lines, no gradients. Dark tech-founder aesthetic. PNG with transparent or black background. Square format.
```

## Notes

- **Size**: Generate at 500x500 or 600x600 — these go INSIDE the 1080x1080 slides, not full-bleed
- **Background**: Always black or transparent so they blend with the slide background
- **Colors**: ONLY orange (#FF6600) and white on black. No blue, no green, no gradients
- **Style**: Flat geometric, minimal, icon-like. Think: tech startup pitch deck illustrations, not detailed art
- **Placement**: Center-bottom area of each slide, between the subtitle text and the divider line, or as a background element with low opacity
