# Arabic Claude MicroSaaS — Folder Guide
## What this folder does
Produces 4 Arabic LinkedIn posts from any transcript:
- 2 posts about AI and Claude in real business
- 2 posts building hype for MicroSaaS training using Claude as operating system
All posts come with visuals, quality scores, pushed to GHL as drafts.
## Files in this folder
- mamoun-founder.md — who Mamoun is, his rules, his voice
- brand-start.md — the trigger and full workflow
- instructions.md — detailed step-by-step process
- skill-arabic-claude-microsaas.md — quality standards and requirements
- good-lookalikes/ — PDF examples to model every post against
- templates/ — approved posts saved here after review
## How to use
1. Open this folder in Cowork
2. Say: "Brand Start"
3. Wait for: "Arabic Claude MicroSaaS loaded. Ready."
4. Paste a transcript (VTT, SRT, plain text, or PDF)
5. Everything else happens automatically
## Output per session
- 4 Arabic posts (2 AI/Claude + 2 MicroSaaS hype)
- 4 visuals (Gemini or Canva based on content)
- 4 quality score tables
- All saved in posts-[DATE] folder
- All saved as drafts in GHL under Mamoun Alamouri personal LinkedIn
- Never published directly — always review first
## Rules that never change
- Jordanian dialect always — not MSA
- No em dashes
- No hype, no guru content, no gimmicks, no direct selling
- Specific numbers and real outcomes only
- Arabic text RTL always — Cairo, Tajawal, Noto Sans Arabic, or Almarai fonts only
- Carousel via Canva, infographic and single visual via Gemini
- Minimum 9/10 quality score before anything ships
- Nothing ships without passing quality gate
## After approval
When a post is approved say:
"This post is approved. Save it as template to Arabic-Claude-MicroSaaS/templates/template-approved.md"
