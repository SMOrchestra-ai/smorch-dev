## PATTERN ANALYSIS — What Makes These Work

**Structure:** Hook (1-2 lines) > Context bridge (2-3 lines) > Numbered sections (3-5) > Closing punch > DM CTA > P.S. question

**Length range:** 1849-2150 characters. Sweet spot is 1800-2200.

**Numbers per post:** Minimum 3 specific data points (91+ companies, $150K/month, 10x lower, $15/click, 300+ features, etc.)

**Named examples per post:** Minimum 1, ideally 2-3 (Unicorn Platform, Tally, Tixee, John Rush, Salesforce, Typeform)

**MENA grounding:** Every post connects to the region. Not forced. Natural integration.

**Voice markers:**
- Short declarative sentences: "Not ads. Not cold outbound. Not partnerships."
- "They" pattern for framework steps: "They don't compete. They localize."
- Direct address: "You write one solid alternatives page in Arabic, you own that keyword."
- Contrast pairs: "Original ideas in, SEO-optimized content out. Not the reverse."
- No em dashes, no hashtags, no emojis, no fluff words

**DM CTA formula:** DM me "[KEYWORD]" + I'll send you [specific deliverable with clear value]

**P.S. formula:** One question that invites a specific personal answer, not a yes/no
