## Style Rules (extracted from approved posts)

- **Hook:** Contrarian or pattern-interrupt opening line
- **Context bridge:** 2-3 lines establishing the gap between common behavior and the better way
- **Sections:** Numbered (1/, 2/, 3/, 4/) with bold sub-headers
- **Proof:** Specific numbers, tools, outcomes (not vague claims)
- **Closing:** Gap statement or mindset shift
- **CTA:** "DM me [KEYWORD]" format
- **P.S.:** Question that invites comment engagement
- **Tone:** Direct, confident, founder-to-founder, zero filler
- **Constraints:** No em dashes, no hashtags, no emojis, under 3000 chars
- **Arrow bullets:** Use → for sub-lists (not dashes or dots)
